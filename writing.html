Traffic Education

Paragraph • BOU Q8
Traffic education is essential for ensuring safety on the roads. It teaches people about traffic rules, signs, and signals. Violation of these rules leads to accidents and traffic jams. If we continue to violate traffic rules, chaos will occur, and lives will be lost. Therefore, everyone, including drivers and pedestrians, must be educated about traffic regulations to prevent accidents and ensure smooth movement.

The Dove and the Ant

Story • BOU Q9
Once upon a time, a dove sat on a tree. The dove saw an ant in the river water. The ant cried for help. The dove was very kind. He wanted to help the ant. He quickly plucked a large leaf and dropped it near the ant. The ant climbed onto the leaf and floated to the bank safely. After a few days, a hunter aimed his arrow at the dove. The ant saw this and bit the hunter's foot. The hunter missed his aim, and the dove flew away. Moral: One good turn deserves another.
