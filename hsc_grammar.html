Survival Grammar

CLOZE & REARRANGE HACKS

Stop guessing randomly.

Cloze tests are math. Look at the word immediately before and after the blank space to know exactly what type of word goes there.

Cloze Test Formulas
The Preposition Rule
Preposition + [ Noun OR Verb+ing ]
If there is a preposition (in, of, for, about) right before the blank, the next word MUST be a Noun or an 'ing' verb.
Example: He is capable of doing this.
The Article Sandwich
Article + [ Adjective ] + Noun
If there is an article (a, an, the) before the blank and a Noun after it, you must put an Adjective in the middle.
Example: It is a beautiful day.
The 'To' Exception
To + [ Base Verb (V1) ]
Unlike other prepositions, 'to' is almost always followed by the basic, present form of a verb. (Except: look forward to, with a view to).
Example: I want to go now.
Rearrange Shortcut
The 5-Step Story Flow
Don't read all 10 sentences at once. Look for sentences in this exact order:
  • 1. Name/Title: Finds the sentence introducing the person/thing.
  • 2. Birth/Origin: "He was born in..."
  • 3. Early Life/Education: What they did growing up.
  • 4. Main Event/Problem: The big thing they are famous for.
  • 5. Conclusion/Death: How it ended.
