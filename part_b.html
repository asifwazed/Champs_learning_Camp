Solved Analysis

Education is the backbone of a nation. No nation can prosper without it.
Why 'prosper'? After the modal verb "can", we need the base form of a verb. The context is about success/growth.
Many people are not aware of the importance of education.
Why 'of'? This is a fixed preposition. "Aware" is almost always followed by "of".
