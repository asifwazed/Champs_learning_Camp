1. Prepositions Tips
• Abide by (rules)
• Absent from (class)
• Based on (truth)
• Capable of (doing)
Memorize these common pairs for Cloze Test.
2. Articles (A/An/The)
• Use 'The' before specific things (The sun, The Padma).
• Use 'An' before vowel sounds (An honest man).
• No article before names of countries (except The USA, The UK).
